Who we are?

We know these are trying times for most businesses and we know despite the situation you are in, you deserve good food. Our platform is designed to bring together farmers and Canadians who have lost good food to this pandemic. We are here to help.

"When you hear about people going hungry and at the same time, food being wasted, it is a very sad situation"

-Umakant Singh

We came up with a innovative and biable solution:
food delivery straigth from the farm

Due to the shutdown of the restaurants that farmers used to sell their food to, farmers lost a lot of food since they did not know who to sell to. Our platform is supposed to provide more buyers for farmers in order to reduce food wastage and loss for farmers as well.

Harvest2Home provides a safer solution to Farmer's Markets for Farmers who have been impacted by Covid-19. Harvest2Home allows individuals and restaurants to deliver fresh food from local farms right to their doorstep.
